ABOUT DR. MARAGA,

David Kenani Maraga is the former chief justice and president of the supreme court of Kenya (2016-2021). Renowned for his integrity and firm stance on judicial independence, he made history in 2017 by leading the supreme court to annul a presidential election - an unprecedented move in Africa that affirmed the strength of Kenya's constitution.
During his tenure, Dr. Maraga championed judicial reforms, transparency and access to justice. His leadership left a lasting impact on Kenya's legal system and set new standards for constitutionalism and accountability in governance.
